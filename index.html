Star Wars

DARTH VADER

Lords of the siths

Dark Vader. Dark Lord of the Sith. Right-hand man of Emperor Palpatine. Ruthless exterminator of the last remaining members of the Jedi Order. A creature that was part man, part machine encased in black armor that became a symbol of fear and death. Few people know that Darth Vader was not always a servant of evil. Before pledging allegiance to Dark Sidious, he was known as Anakin Skywalker, a brave Jedi Knight of the Old Republic and lover of Padmé Amidala. While the Clone Wars were still raging and the young Anakin fought tirelessly to defend the Republic alongside his master and friend, Obi-Wan Kenobi, the young Knight was plagued by increasingly frequent terrible visions in which he witnessed, powerless, the death of his beloved Padmé Amidala, with whom he had secretly married.
